Martian Surface Explorer

Hello! This project is based on the idea of a surface-exploring robot, that can do two primary things:

  • Pathfind the way to a given destination given a map
  • For unexplored terrain, map out a given area

In each case, the user (you) can pick a certain number of pathfinding/mapping algorithms available, and in case of the pathfinder you can also choose a parameter you want to optimise for: either the Energy Consumption of the rover while travelling, or the Time Taken by the rover to travel.

As the Martian terrain is not just walls existing in thin air, the map given to the user is a contour map.
